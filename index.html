Austagram, Kishoreganj
Kishoreganj — Bangladesh's পনির heartland

The Austagram region of Kishoreganj has a centuries-long tradition of dairy craftsmanship. The fertile floodplains and rich milk from local cattle make this region produce some of the finest পনির in Bangladesh. La Maison was founded to bring this heritage directly to Dhaka.
